Courses: Adults
Savvy Signing
Students will learn basic features of American Sign Language such as expressive and receptive finger spelling, referencing, basic vocabulary, parameters, sentence structure (OSV) and some other grammatical features vital to developing fluency. Some videos, handouts and interactive websites will be incorporated into the lessons to hone skills through practice with assigned partners. (No purchase of a text is required but, many of the lessons will be drawn from A Basic Course in American Sign Language, Second Edition which can be purchased on Amazon at a minimal cost to students.).
When: July 11 - August 3, Tuesdays and Thursdays, 11:00am - 1:00pm

Electric Vehicle Course
Electric Cars 101. All you need to know about electric vehicles, the fundamentals and hybrid safety. Be able to identify the HAZARDS in the workplace, learn about hybrid vehicle principles, vehicle parts, and explore different types of hybrid vehicles and understand hybrid operations. Open discussions on hybrid operation and new vehicle technologies.
When: July 10 - July 13 or July 17 - July 20, 9:00am - 12:00pm
